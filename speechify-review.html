Speechify Review 2026: Is the $139/Year Premium Worth It?

Updated May 2026 · 8 min read
Bottom line: Speechify is the best tool for consuming written content as audio. If you read a lot of articles, documents, or books, it genuinely saves hours per week.
Rating: 4.2/5 — Best for: Professionals who consume large volumes of text, students, anyone with reading-heavy workflows

What Is Speechify?

Speechify is different from most tools on this site. While Murf AI and ElevenLabs are production tools for creating voiceover content, Speechify is a consumption tool — it converts any written content into audio so you can listen rather than read.

The pitch is simple: you can listen at 2-3x speed while doing other things, consuming the same amount of content in a fraction of the time. For people with reading-heavy jobs, research workflows, or large document queues, the time savings are real.

Key Features

Speed Control

Speechify's standout feature is aggressive speed control. The tool supports listening speeds up to 4.5x, with a natural-sounding voice even at high speeds. Most users settle at 2-3x after a short adjustment period. At 3x speed, a 20-minute article takes under 7 minutes.

Cross-Platform Sync

Start an article on your desktop and pick it up exactly where you left off on mobile. The sync is reliable and near-instant. Browser extension, iOS, Android, and Mac app all share the same queue and position.

Content Import

Speechify handles virtually any text source: PDFs, web articles (via browser extension), Google Docs, Dropbox files, emails, and direct text paste. The browser extension is particularly useful — one click converts any webpage to audio.

AI Voices

The premium voice selection includes 30+ high-quality AI voices. The voices are clear and pleasant at normal speeds; quality holds surprisingly well at 2x. Above 3x, some voices start sounding slightly unnatural — this is normal for any TTS at extreme speeds.

The pricing is straightforward. Either the free plan is enough for casual use, or you need Premium for the full feature set. There's no middle tier.

Is $139/Year Worth It?

The honest answer: it depends entirely on how much text you consume. If you read 5+ articles per day, review long documents regularly, or study from text materials, the time savings at 2-3x speed easily justify $139/year. If you occasionally want to listen to an article while commuting, the free plan may be sufficient.

The break-even calculation: if Speechify saves you 30 minutes per day, that's over 180 hours per year. At any professional hourly rate, $139 pays for itself many times over.

What Speechify Doesn't Do

Speechify is not a voiceover production tool. You cannot use it to generate audio for YouTube videos, podcasts, or any content creation purpose. For that, you need Murf AI or ElevenLabs. Speechify is purely for personal consumption of existing text content.
